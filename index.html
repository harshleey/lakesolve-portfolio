About Me

I'm a product designer with over 4 years of experience creating digital products that balance business goals and user needs. My approach combines strategic thinking with detailed execution to deliver meaningful experiences.

I worked with various startups and tech companies to refine my digital product design skills. I've had the privilege of working with clients across fintech, healthcare, and Real Estate sectors.
