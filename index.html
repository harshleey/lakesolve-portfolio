Cosub App

Entertainment Solutions

Duration

4 months

Role

Lead Product
Designer

Challenge

With rising subscription costs in Nigeria and limited
multi-user management options, users struggle to:

  • Manage multiple subscriptions across platforms.
  • Remember renewal dates and payment deadlines.
  • Share and split subscription costs easily with friends or family.
  • This leads to over-payments, expired plans, and wasted funds.
Process/ Possible Solution

To design an intuitive platform that:

  • Simplifies subscription sharing — allowing one user (the sharer) to split access securely.
  • Centralizes subscription management — reminders, status updates, and payments all in one app.
  • Improves affordability — enabling users to pay less for premium digital experiences.
